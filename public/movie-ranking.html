The Best Movies of all time..

My top 4 movies of all time.

1. The Vampire Diaries.

"The Vampire Diaries" related to supernatural (vampirism).It is a pretty enormous series with 8 seasons, each containing 22 episodes per season, but it's worth watching.

2. Money Heist

"Money Heist" is a Spanish TV series about a group of robbers who plan and execute elaborate heists, led by "The Professor," blending drama, action, and suspense.

3. Lucifer

"Lucifer" is a TV series following Lucifer Morningstar, the Devil, who abandons Hell for Los Angeles, running a nightclub and assisting the LAPD in solving crimes.

4. Reacher

"Reacher" is a TV series based on Lee Child's novels, featuring Jack Reacher, a former military policeman solving crimes and delivering justice with his keen intellect and physical prowess.

5. Mirzapur

"Mirzapur" is an Indian web series set in the lawless town of Mirzapur, Uttar Pradesh, revolving around crime, power struggles, and the mafia, with complex characters and intense drama.
